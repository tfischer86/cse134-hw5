Welcome!

Hello and welcome to my portfolio site! My name is Thomas Fischer and I'm a computer science student with a passion for open source development and software engineering.

I am currently working on my B.S. in Computer Science from UC San Diego.

As an active contributor to open source projects, I have worked on many high-profile projects like Vim and i3. My contributions include fixing bugs, implementing new features and improving the user experience of these projects.

I have over 5 years of experience in Java, C, C++ and Python. I am also familiar with web development and have worked with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I am interested in many fields of programming, including machine learning, real time graphics, networking, and multithreaded programming.

Apart from programming, I am also interested in music and art. I am an avid reader and love to learn about new technologies and advancements in the field.

My ultimate career goal is to become a software engineer at a leading tech company where I can work on challenging and innovative projects. I hope to contribute to open source projects in my free time, helping to create software that benefits people around the world.
